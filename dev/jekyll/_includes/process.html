UX Process

Long-term, sustainable user experience design is an iterative, cyclical process. Design sprints will almost always start with ideation. Analysis & assessment will then occur, followed by more ideation, strategy planning, & implementation. Testing & iteration will begin towards the end of the sprint, & will continue long after the sprint is over. As a company's business goals evolve, & you gain more data & insight about your users & their behavior, the products & features you are designing will evolve as well.
